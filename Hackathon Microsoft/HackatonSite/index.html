A new way to clean your DESK.

You are tired to organize your desk or, you are just lazy?

Don't worry, with our sofware you will be able to organize and improve your work environment.

MAKE YOUR OFFICE GREAT AGAIN

SMARTDESK idea has been created during the Hackaton of TECH'OFFICE : make your office great again.


The idea

The idea is simply to help you to improve, organize your environment in order to increase ergonomics, your efficiency and your wellness.

In the future

You would be able to interact with your desk; organize it in the way you like with VR or Drag&Drop, save your configuration, display a calendar, a calculator, or anything you could need to help you!

More about...

Our product

Let's introduce SMARTDESK software.

SMARTDESK software is a JAVA application made in 1 day, the features are very easy but the software is far to be complete.

Basic features

  • Choose which background you want to work on.
  • Select the accessoiries you would like to have on your desk.
  • The software will display the most ergonomics desk according to your selections.
  • Place the accessoiries on your desk.
  • Enjoyed your new organised desk !
